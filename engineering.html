Engineering Evolution Timeline

The Pre-Dynastic Era (Before 3100 BC)

Early Egyptians started with mud-brick engineering, creating the first irrigation systems to control the Nile floods.

The Old Kingdom (The Pyramid Age)

This era saw the transition from mud-brick to massive stone masonry. Imhotep changed history by building the Step Pyramid.
